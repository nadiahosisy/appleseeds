Setter

The setter is responsible for receiving the ball from the initial pass and setting it up for a teammate to attack. They play a crucial role in orchestrating the team's offense by making precise and strategic passes. For more info visit wikipedia. Or Another Sport Site.

>

Outside Hitter

Also known as the left-side hitter, the outside hitter is positioned on the left side of the net. They are usually the primary attackers and play a key role in both serving and blocking. Outside hitters need to possess strong hitting and defensive skills. For more info visit wikipedia.

>

Middle Blocker

Positioned in the center of the net, the middle blocker plays a crucial role in both offense and defense. They are responsible for blocking the opponent's attacks, as well as executing quick attacks from the center of the net. Middle blockers need to have excellent blocking and jumping abilities.
